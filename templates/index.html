Image2Latex

Convert an image of a formula to MathJax Syntax

FAQ

How do I use this?

Just upload a screenshot of a LaTeX/MathJax formula and we'll try to convert it into LaTeX/MathJax syntax!

Why would I use this?

Honestly, we're not too sure. MathJax usually isn't supported by typical OCR (optical character recognition) engines and this can help with converting pdfs to LaTeX documents.

How did you make this?

This front end was made with Bootstrap, and the backend uses Flask and PyTorch. We made this together for the Ignition Hacks 2021 Hackathon.
